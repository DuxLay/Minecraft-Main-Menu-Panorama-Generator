The Ultimate Lightweight Tool for Minecraft Main Menu Panoramas

With the help of AI collaborators like Claude and Gemini, I went through the process of learning, debugging, and refining until I created this, my very first piece of software. I’ve open-sourced it so that other content creators and Minecraft fans can save time and focus on what really matters: their creativity.

🚀 What is DuxLay 360 Splitter?
DuxLay 360 Splitter is a clean, modern, and efficient utility designed to take any 360° panoramic image (equirectangular projection) and instantly slice it into the six required cubic faces (panorama_0 through panorama_5) needed for Minecraft custom resource packs.

It is designed for creators who want to transform their game’s title screen into a truly immersive experience without the headache of manual cropping or complex image manipulation software.

✨ Key Features
Instant Conversion: Just load your 360° image, and the tool will automatically handle the slicing, orientation, and perfect grid layout required by Minecraft.

Modern UI: A clean, user-friendly interface that includes an automatic Dark Mode, ensuring your eyes stay comfortable while you work.

Multilingual Support: Seamlessly switch between Spanish and English to suit your workflow.

Zero Dependencies Required: Since this is a portable tool, you don't need to install complex libraries or heavy compilers; just run the .exe and start creating.

Lightweight: Designed to be extremely efficient, even on low-end systems.

🛠️ How it Works
Load: Select your favorite 360° panorama image file.

Split: Click the convert button. The tool will generate the six faces needed for the Minecraft main menu background.

Apply: Take the generated files and place them directly into your resource pack's directory.

🛡️ Windows SmartScreen / Anti-Virus Notice
Since this is an open-source tool and does not carry a costly commercial digital certificate, Windows SmartScreen might display an "Unknown Publisher" or "Unsafe file" warning when launching the .exe for the first time.
